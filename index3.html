Contact us

At O Logistics, our mission is to provide reliable and efficient logistics services to our customers, delivering their goods by land, air, and sea. We strive to be the preferred logistics partner for businesses of all sizes, providing end-to-end supply chain solutions that meet their unique needs and exceed their expectations.

Our Values:

  • Integrity: We are committed to conducting our business with the highest levels of integrity and honesty. We believe in building long-term relationships with our customers, suppliers, and employees based on trust and transparency.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do, from the quality of our services to the professionalism of our staff. We invest in the latest technology and training to ensure that our services are always at the cutting edge of the industry.
  • Customer Focus: Our customers are at the center of everything we do. We listen carefully to their needs and work tirelessly to provide solutions that meet their unique requirements. We believe that by putting our customers first, we can build lasting relationships that benefit everyone involved.
  • Innovation: We are always looking for new and better ways to deliver our services. We encourage our staff to think creatively and to embrace change, so that we can continue to evolve and improve as a company.
  • Responsibility: We take our responsibility to the environment and our communities seriously. We are committed to minimizing our impact on the environment and to being a good corporate citizen wherever we operate.

Our Services:

Land:

We offer a comprehensive range of land transportation services, including full truckload, less-than-truckload, and intermodal solutions. Our extensive network of carriers and our state-of-the-art tracking and monitoring systems ensure that our customers' goods are delivered safely and on time, every time.

Air:

We provide air freight services to destinations around the world. Our global network of partners and our experienced team of airfreight specialists ensure that our customers' shipments are delivered quickly and efficiently, regardless of their size or destination.

Sea:

Our ocean freight services include full container load, less-than-container load, and break-bulk solutions. Our experienced team of ocean freight specialists can manage every aspect of our customers' shipments, from pick-up to delivery, and our global network of partners ensures that we can reach any destination in the world.

Warehousing and Distribution:

We offer a range of warehousing and distribution services, including inventory management, order fulfillment, and cross-docking. Our facilities are equipped with the latest technology and are staffed by experienced professionals who are dedicated to providing the highest levels of service.

Customs Brokerage:

We provide customs brokerage services to ensure that our customers' shipments clear customs smoothly and efficiently. Our experienced team of customs specialists can handle even the most complex customs requirements, ensuring that our customers' goods are delivered without delay.

Conclusion:

At O Logistics, we are committed to providing our customers with the highest levels of service and reliability. Whether ourrs need to move goods by land, air, or sea, we have the experience, the expertise, and the global network to get the job done. We are proud of our reputation as a trusted logistics partner, and we look forward to continuing to serve our customers for many years to come.
